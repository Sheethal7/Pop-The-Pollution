How do you play the game?

  • This is a two player game called Air Rescue!
  • The main objective of the game is to catch as many people who are falling from the plane before they drop to the ground.
  • The first player is the purple character on the left side of the screen who you can move using the keys a and d.
  • The second player is the red character on the right side of the screen who you can move using the left and right arrow keys.
  • You can choose which level you want to play in the next screen.
  • Each woman caught is a 1 point, each man caught are two points and each kid caught are three points.
  • Each person dropped is negative 1 point.
  • There is also a timer of 60s

This game is not meant to offend anyone, this is for purely entertainment purposes only.
