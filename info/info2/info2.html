These Gases Affect The Earth In Many Ways

  • Carbon Dioxide(CO2), Methane(CH4), Nitrous Oxide(NO2), CFCs and HFCs are all greenhouse gases and when produced in bulk these cause Global Warming(The warming of the Earth Temperature causing melting of polar ice caps, increase in sea levels, etc)
  • Sulfur Dioxide(SO2), Nitrogen Oxides(N2O, NO, NO2, NO5) causes acid rain, which can damage ecosystems, buildings, and soil. It also contributes to respiratory issues like asthma and bronchitis.
  • Nitrogen Oxides (N2O, NO, NO2, NO5)
  • Carbon Monoxide(CO), Hydrogen Sulfide (H₂S) and Ammonia (NH₃) are toxic gases that can lead to serious health problems when inhaled, particularly affecting the cardiovascular, nervous and respiratory systems.

These gases are released by industries, vehicles, burning of fossil fuels, refrigerators among others.
