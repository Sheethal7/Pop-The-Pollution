Air Pollution is Caused by Many Different Gases

  • Carbon Dioxide (CO2)
  • Sulfur Dioxide (SO2)
  • Nitrogen Oxides (N2O, NO, NO2, NO5)
  • Methane (CH4)
  • Carbon Monoxide (CO)
  • Hydrogen Sulphide (H2S)
  • Ammonia (NH3)
  • CFCs and HFCs

These gases are released by industries, vehicles, burning of fossil fuels, refrigerators among others.
